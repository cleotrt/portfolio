The NeoConsortium //



I did a two-and-a-half-month internship at NeoConsortium, a fictitious multinational presenting itself as a leader in the contemporary art market, before continuing to work for them as a graphic designer. They propose a critical approach to the capitalist excesses of today’s world. Their artistic proposals take the form of irregular polyhedrons, depending on the nature of the work. Discovered in 2022 at the NEMO Biennial at CENTQUATRE PARIS, their work is based on a highly immersive narrative, blurring the lines between reality and fiction.

The Ministry of Culture’s Mondes Nouveaux project //

The French Ministry of Culture’s post-covid cultural revival project is a call for projects on the theme of «New Worlds». The NeoConsortium project was selected along with 26O other highly diverse artistic projects. The exhibition takes place at 104 PARIS for the biennale of digital arts, from September to January 2024.
